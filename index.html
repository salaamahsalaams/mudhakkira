اقْرَأْ بِاسْمِ رَبِّكَ الَّذِي خَلَقَ

We live in an age of endless content. Most of it passes through and leaves nothing behind — a lecture watched, a book opened, a video that felt important in the moment and was forgotten by evening.

These notes are the antidote to that. Whenever something is worth consuming, it gets turned into a structured, readable page — Arabic preserved, key points distilled, takeaways clear. Free to read. Free to download. Free to share.

If it helped, pass it on.

وَاللَّهُ يَعْلَمُ وَأَنتُمْ لَا تَعْلَمُونَ
